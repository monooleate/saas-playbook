## Practices to expand

- Time-to-value is the metric. Measure it.
- Reduce form fields by 50%; conversion goes up by ~25% (industry
  rule of thumb).
- Show progress; people finish what they start.
- Don't ask for credit card unless your model needs it.
- Show, don't tell — animated GIF beats paragraph of text.
- The first 60 seconds matter more than the next 60 minutes.
