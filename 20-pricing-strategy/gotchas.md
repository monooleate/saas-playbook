# 20 — Pricing Strategy — Gotchas

> **Status:** Outline.

## Categories

1. **Underpricing forever.** Set a price month 1, never raise it.
   Compounds into massive opportunity cost.
2. **Tier shuffling that screws existing customers.** Renamed Pro to
   Plus, removed feature; existing customers feel cheated.
3. **Currency arbitrage.** PPP pricing in cheap markets; users in
   expensive markets sign up via VPN.
4. **Per-seat encourages account sharing.** Customers share one login
   to dodge the seat charge.
5. **Per-usage panic.** Customer hits unexpected limit; outraged
   support ticket. Always show upcoming usage prominently.
6. **A/B price-test backlash.** Different visitors saw different
   prices, screenshots leaked, trust damaged.
7. **Free tier costs more than it brings.** Hosting, support,
   abuse — without conversion to paid.

(Real incidents in v0.2.)
