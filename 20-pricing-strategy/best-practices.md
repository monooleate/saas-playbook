## Practices

- You are underpriced. You.
- Anchor high; discount visibly.
- Annual is a feature for the customer (lock in price) and for you
  (cash, retention).
- Grandfather. Always.
- Public pricing wins for self-serve.
- Test message, layout, anchor — not the price itself, until you have
  data.
- Currency localization > PPP for most cases.
