# 16 — Customer Support & Changelog — Best Practices

> **Status:** Outline.

## Sources

- Help Scout — "Support Driven" content.
- Linear's product team blog posts on changelog publishing.

## Practices

- Reply within 24h, even if "we're looking into this." Silence kills.
- Templates save time but personalize the first sentence.
- Changelog is for users, not for your CV. Lead with benefit.
- Public roadmap commits to nothing; that's its value.
- Status page updates are a public trust transaction; act fast.
