# 15 — Legal & Compliance — Best Practices

> **Status:** Outline.

## Sources

- Iubenda guides.
- GDPR.eu.
- Stripe / Notion / Linear ToS as readable templates.

## Practices

- Plain language. Lawyers can write defensible legalese; you write
  for trust.
- Versioning is non-optional. Old contracts must be retrievable.
- Material changes require notice + re-consent.
- Subprocessor list public; customers can opt out (in spirit; many
  can't really).
- Cookie banner only if you actually use non-essential cookies.
  Most SaaS don't, if you skip Google Analytics.
