# 15 — Legal & Compliance — Checklist

> **Status:** Outline.

- ToS published at `/terms`. Versioned (`v1.0` in URL or footer).
- Privacy Policy at `/privacy`. Versioned.
- Footer links to both on every page.
- Cookie banner if non-essential cookies set.
- Consent record schema in DB (when, IP, version accepted).
- Subprocessor list public at `/subprocessors`.
- DPA template available on request (PDF).
- GDPR data export endpoint + UI button.
- GDPR account delete endpoint + UI button.
- Email confirmation on both.
- Account delete fully removes within 30 days; document the path.
- Lawyer reviewed before launch (date noted).
- ToS / Privacy review cadence: annually or when material changes.
- VAT / tax handling per topic 03 jurisdiction notes.
- Data residency stated (EU customers' data in EU regions).
