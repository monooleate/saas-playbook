# 17 — Marketing Site & SEO — Checklist

> **Status:** Outline.

## Foundation

- Marketing site as a separate workspace in the monorepo (or a
  separate repo).
- Static generation (no server-side rendering for marketing pages).
- Lighthouse 90+ across all four metrics.
- Mobile-friendly (passes Google's mobile-friendly test).

## SEO basics

- `<title>` and `<meta description>` per page.
- OG images per page.
- `sitemap.xml` generated and submitted to Google Search Console.
- `robots.txt` allowing crawling.
- Canonical URLs set.
- Schema.org JSON-LD for Organization, SaaSProduct, FAQPage.

## Content

- Home page with one clear value prop.
- Pricing page (public).
- Features section with screenshots.
- Vs-competitor pages for top 2–3 competitors.
- Blog with at least 5 cornerstone articles.
- Programmatic SEO landing pages (if applicable).

## Operational

- Google Search Console set up.
- Bing Webmaster Tools set up.
- Plausible / Umami analytics (privacy-friendly, no cookie banner).
- Monthly review of top pages, click-through rates.
