# 24 — Launch Playbook — Best Practices

> **Status:** Outline.

## Sources

- Pieter Levels' Twitter / NomadList launch.
- Indie Hackers post-mortems.
- Product Hunt's launch guide.
- *Tribes* — Seth Godin (community-driven launches).

## Practices

- Build the audience before the product.
- Beta is for product fixes, not marketing.
- One channel per launch day — depth > breadth.
- Be present. Reply to every comment.
- Don't promise; ship.
- Launch is a moment; growth is a habit.
