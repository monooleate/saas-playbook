# 24 — Launch Playbook — Gotchas

> **Status:** Outline.

## Categories

1. **Server falls over on launch day.** Cap costs (topic 01 G-10) but
   also: pre-warm, stress-test once.
2. **Stripe Tax not yet registered in target jurisdictions.** First
   sales fail compliance.
3. **Subreddit rules violated.** Account banned mid-launch.
4. **Product Hunt hunter goes silent at launch hour.** Co-launch only
   with someone who has a track record.
5. **Demo video has a typo / wrong product name.** Re-recording in 2h
   = stress; pre-review with one friend.
6. **Email sent to waitlist with broken link.** Sanity-check every
   send.
7. **"Show HN" downvoted because over-promotional.** Lead with the
   technical problem, not the marketing.

(Real incidents in v0.2.)
