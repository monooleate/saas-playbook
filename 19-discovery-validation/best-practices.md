## Practices

- Talk to customers before, during, and after build.
- Interviews about past behavior > opinions about future behavior.
- Pre-orders > waitlists > "I'd pay for that."
- The market lies. Behavior tells the truth.
- Kill before scale, not after.
- One customer who pays >> ten who say they will.
