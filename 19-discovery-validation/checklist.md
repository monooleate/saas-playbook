# 19 — Discovery & Validation — Checklist

> **Status:** Outline.

## Problem validation

- Written problem statement — one sentence, naming who hurts and how.
- 10 customer interviews completed using The Mom Test rules.
- Notes from each interview saved (not from memory; recorded with consent).
- Pattern analysis: what came up unprompted in 5+ of 10 interviews?

## Competitive analysis

- List of 5–10 existing tools that solve the same or adjacent problem.
- For each: pricing, target customer, what they do well, what they
  don't, why a customer would switch.
- "Why now" thesis written.

## Smoke test

- Landing page live with one CTA.
- Paid traffic ($50–$200) driven.
- Conversion measured; recorded.
- A/B test of value prop messaging if conversion < 2%.

## Pre-MVP commitment

- 3–10 customers on a waitlist who replied to a personal email.
- 1+ customer pre-paying or signing an LOI (B2B).

## MVP scope

- One-page MVP scope document.
- Three "must have" features. Everything else is "later".
- Done definition for MVP: what does shipped look like?

## Kill criteria

- Time-box defined (e.g. 12 weeks).
- Quantitative goal defined (e.g. 3 paying customers by week 12).
- Honesty pact: if criteria not met, decide pivot/persevere/kill.
