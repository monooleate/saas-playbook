# 19 — Discovery & Validation — Gotchas

> **Status:** Outline.

## Categories

1. **Building because validation said "interesting".** Interest ≠
   commitment. Money ≠ talk.
2. **Selection bias in interviews.** Talking to friends and Twitter
   followers — not the actual buyer.
3. **Asking leading questions.** "Wouldn't it be great if X?" → yes.
   Worthless data.
4. **Sunk-cost ignoring kill criteria.** Six months in, no customers,
   "but we've come so far."
5. **Smoke test conversion driven by curiosity, not buying intent.**
   "Free" funnel doesn't validate willingness to pay.
6. **Validating a feature, not a problem.** People want X but won't
   pay for X by itself.
7. **Confusing audience size with market size.** 1M tweets ≠ 1M
   buyers.

(Real incidents in v0.2.)
